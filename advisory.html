03

Thin & Modular Precast System for Quick Pavement Rehabilitation

Designed an innovative modular and thin precast ECC slab system (3.4×2.4×0.12m) at a heavily trafficked Tampines intersection under the LTA and JTC Corporation project. ECC mixed with GGBS provides a decarbonization solution with higher performance. A ductile yet provides a high skid resistance pavement. Road reopened to traffic within two days. A solution with lower lifecycle cost, low carbon, and significantly extended service life in comparison with conventional pavement.

04

Study Investigation of Railway-Noise

Investigation on high noise emission along several MRT lines in Singapore. Methodology included comparative study of rail corrugation, radius of railway tracks, train operations, track quality, and friction modification. Identified parameters contributing to high noise levels and proposed targeted improvements: rail roughness correction, gauge adjustment, friction modification, rail and wheel damping, and fastening system adjustments.

05

Design and Construction of Cast-In Situ Engineered Cementitious Composite Pavement

Design and construction of a continuous pavement made of 130mm thickness of Strain Hardening Cementitious Composites (SHCC) slab for road premises. The innovative design is to tackle existing long-term structural cracks and plastic deformation issues. Ultra-ductile SHCC mixed with GGBS delivers a long-lasting pavement performance with low-maintenance for heavy-vehicle industrial environments. Further, the modified SHCC is a low carbon construction material.
