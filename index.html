Focus

Green Infrastructure & Sustainable Transport

Three main specialties addressed across system, innovation, and technical levels.

Our expertise is centered on Green Infrastructure and Sustainable Transport, with main specialties on three key infrastructure domains. Those three specialties are and can be addressed through different levels including system & policy, innovation, and technical implementation.

1
Roadway / Highway Infrastructure
Pavement engineering, highway design, material construction, structural performance, functional performance, geometric design, drainage systems, modular and precast system
2
Land Transport System
Transport planning, Transit-Oriented Development, electromobility, MRT/BRT systems, Nature Based Solution Infrastructure, Traffic Impact Analysis
3
Railway Infrastructure
Rail track engineering, rail-noise mitigation, photovoltaics-noise barrier railway, and energy harvesting railway infrastructure
